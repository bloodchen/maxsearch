Browser issues explained

These pages explain why certain browser behaviors occur, focused on the underlying technical limitations and design trade-offs of modern web browsers. They are intended as a neutral reference to help understand the mechanics of browser operations rather than provide step-by-step fixes or product support.

Each explanation is meant to be read independently. Users should visit the specific page that matches the behavior or issue they have encountered for detailed technical context.
